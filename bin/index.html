Welcome to the place where ideas meet action

We believe creating amazing brand relationships is about creating culture; it’s about creating things that people want to bring themselves to, rather than interruptions they endure.

Luckily for us, New York is the cultural capital of the world. When we look out windows, when speak in the elevator, when walk out for lunch, it is waiting, it is stirring, it is staring us in the face. It’s unavoidable. And when you throw our personalities into the mix, it’s what makes WKNY different – this combination of cultural immersion and unique people. Few of us are actually from New York. We come from farms, cities and far away countries. We, this group of permanent visitors, call each other friends and we call this office home. We are designers, thinkers, bakers, painters, poets and parents. While our histories are varied, our goal at WKNY is the same: to make the best work of our entire lives. But we do not work in the singular thread of one mind. We combine. Our talents. Our thoughts. Our dreams. And when we combine, our ideas combust. They become things – positive cultural contributions. And we hope they change everything for the better. Welcome to the place where ideas meet action. Welcome to the office that defies description in the city that defines the world. This is Wieden + Kennedy. This is New York. This is Wieden + Kennedy New York.
